The Hog Rider is a Rare card that is unlocked from the Builder's Workshop (Arena 5). It spawns a fast, single-target, building-targeting, melee, ground troop with high hitpoints and high damage.

He is able to jump over the river. He appears as a dark-skinned shirtless man with blue eyes, dark brown eyebrows, beard and mohawk, and a golden body piercing in his left ear, wearing a team-colored belt and wristbands, along with a brown loincloth and sandals.

He wields a hammer with two brass-colored bands and a team-colored ribbon tied at the bottom, and is riding a hog. A Hog Rider card costs 4 Elixir to deploy.
